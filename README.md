# Echo

A blockchain-secured ledger for communities to log, own, and trade the slang, inside jokes, and games they invented.

Every generation creates language faster than any institution can document it. Every friend group has expressions and games that exist nowhere in writing and disappear when the group drifts apart. Echo makes that ownership legible, tradeable, and permanent.

## The anchor use case

ASL communities evolve language faster than any centralized body can track. Echo gives signing communities the infrastructure for rapid community-level language creation and adoption. Your community invented a new sign for something that has no word yet. It is yours. Echo makes sure it stays that way.

## How it works

- Communities submit expressions, games, and inside language to the ledger
- Each entry is attributed to its origin with a block hash and timestamp
- Ownership is permanent and transferable
- The ledger is public and tamper-proof

## Expansion

Any subculture with a shared lexicon worth owning. The ASL anchor is the proof of concept. The platform scales to every community that invents faster than the culture can document.
